Eyetrip: A Dual-Platform Immersive Media Ecosystem for the Web3 Creative Future

1. Executive Summary

Eyetrip is a visionary two-part digital platform that reimagines how immersive media is experienced, collected, and monetized. Through Eyetrip Images, an ultra-optimized portfolio site showcasing high-fidelity visuals, and Eyetrip VR, a WebXR experience built for standalone headsets and desktop VR, the project delivers a complete ecosystem for storytelling, showcasing, and interacting with digital art.

Designed by award-winning creative technologist Dulce Baerga and visionary artist David Aughenbaugh, Eyetrip is built on open web technologies, integrated with streaming pipelines and upcoming Web3 wallet features, and engineered to support scalable monetization through marketplace and licensing integrations.

2. Project Vision

Eyetrip bridges the gap between artist portfolios, immersive experience design, and Web3-native commerce by offering:

  • A performant, scalable platform to showcase high-res images and 360¬∞ video
  • Seamless VR/WebXR deployment for immersive storytelling
  • Future-facing integration of blockchain wallet features and NFT-ready marketplace tools
  • A collaborative partnership model between technical and artistic leads to expand into LBE (location-based experiences) and competition circuits

3. Technical Architecture Overview

A. Eyetrip Images (eyetripimages.com)

  • Framework: Next.js / Vercel CDN / Tailwind CSS
  • Media Tools: Custom image galleries with Gigapixel viewer
  • SEO: Pre-rendered routes, custom metadata, fast Lighthouse scores
  • CMS (in progress): Headless CMS for blog/newsletter/marketplace

B. Eyetrip VR (eyetripvr.com)

  • Framework: Three.js r170 + WebXR API + Vanilla JavaScript
  • Optimized for: Meta Quest (90FPS goal), Desktop VR/WebGL, Mobile iOS/Android
  • Media: Embedded 360¬∞ spatial video streaming with adaptive quality (360p-4K)
  • VR Interface: Custom menu interactions, controller-based navigation, interactive audio hotspots
  • Deployment: Progressive Web App with VR headset support
  • Future: Wallet login, NFT drops, interactive scenes

This project includes comprehensive infrastructure, streaming optimization, interface design, QA, animations, multi-device compatibility, and future-facing integrations across both platforms.

6. Future Roadmap

A. Technical

  • Web3 wallet login (MetaMask, WalletConnect)
  • Token-gated experiences + smart contract integration
  • NFT marketplace (custom + 3rd-party integrations)
  • Expanded CMS for writing, shop, and publishing
  • Local device caching for XR optimization

B. Marketing & Business

  • Submit to LBE/VR art festivals (Tribeca Immersive, IDFA DocLab, SIGGRAPH XR, A Maze, Venice VR)
  • Launch e-commerce campaign (limited edition prints, VR tickets, NFT collectibles)
  • SEO-optimized content marketing (blog, social)
  • Monthly newsletter for audience building and cross-promotion
  • Partner with galleries for spatial installations

7. Strategic Partnership Model

The development model behind Eyetrip is a co-owned creative partnership that trades upfront capital for shared equity, where:

  • Artistic Lead handles visual content, branding, and audience-building
  • Technical Lead builds, deploys, and maintains the XR infrastructure
  • Revenue will be shared 50/50 across LBE earnings, online marketplace sales, and competition prizes

8. Conclusion

Eyetrip is more than a portfolio. It is a foundation for a creative economy that spans immersive narrative, WebXR storytelling, and blockchain-enabled monetization. This white paper anchors our vision and supports the broader roadmap for Eyetrip's expansion into XR, art, and digital commerce.
